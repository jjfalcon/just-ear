JustEar Demo

This sample merges two different mono audio streams (Adele & Michael Jackson) to a stereo audio stream (left is Adele, right is Michael Jackson) and sends it to JustEar Media Server. You can play the stream below and hear Adele on the left, Michael Jackson on the right. Use your headphones to hear audio clearly in different channels.


  1. Click the "Publish" button to publish local audio to JustEar Media Server


    2. Status:Offline



  2. Click the "Play" button to play remote audio coming from JustEar Media Server


    3. Use your headphones to listen audio in different channels
